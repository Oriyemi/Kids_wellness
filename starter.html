    FAQs

    Are the products bundled in the Starter Kit vegan?

    The KOA Kids Starter Kit bundles all three of our products: Tummy Time, Boost, and Grow+Thrive.

    Tummy Time and Boost are 100% vegan. None of the ingredients come from animals, and no animal-derived materials are used at any point in sourcing or manufacturing.

    Grow+Thrive doesn’t contain any animal ingredients in the final product. However, we’re all about transparency, so here’s the scoop: the natural raspberry flavour is made using ethanol that was originally derived from a cheese byproduct. That ethanol is completely removed during the manufacturing process and isn't present in the final vitamin.

    What makes this combo better than a regular multivitamin?

    Each product in the Starter Kit plays a unique role:

  • Grow+Thrive delivers 18 essential nutrients + 9 fruits and veggies to support energy, growth, and brain health.
  • Boost is packed with Vitamin C, D3, Zinc, Elderberry & Kakadu Plum to help defend against bugs and boost immune resilience.
  • Tummy Time supplies 20 strains of probiotics + prebiotics to support digestion, regularity, and immunity from the inside out.

    • Together, they support total wellness—gut, growth, and glow.

    What ages are KOA products suitable for?

    Our products are designed for kids aged 2 and up. Each formula is carefully dosed for growing bodies, and we always recommend supervising younger children while chewing.

    Is the Starter Kit suitable for daily, year-round use?

    As with all things health-related, it’s always best to check in with your healthcare professional first, but yes! All three products are formulated for ongoing daily use. They’re designed to support kids through school terms, growth spurts, travel days, and everything in between.

    What if my child doesn’t like one of the products?

    Each formula is naturally flavoured and kid-approved—but if your little one is picky, try making it part of a daily ritual (hello, KOA vitamin dance!). You can also mix Tummy Time into their fave cold food or drink for easy wins.

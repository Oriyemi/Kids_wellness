FAQs

What makes KOA Kids different from other vitamin brands?

KOA Kids combines cutting-edge nutritional science with serious fun—no added sugar, no artificial colours, flavours, or sweeteners. Just powerful, science-backed ingredients in formats kids actually want to take. We make healthy habits easy, playful, and parent-approved.

Are KOA Kids vitamins safe?

All KOA products are made in Australia in TGA-licensed facilities and are listed with the Therapeutic Goods Administration (TGA), meaning they meet strict standards for quality, manufacturing, and evidence-based ingredients. They're designed for kids aged 2 and up, using clean ingredients chosen with care for growing bodies.

What ages are KOA products suitable for?

Our products are designed for kids aged 2 and up. Each formula is carefully dosed for growing bodies, and we always recommend supervising younger children while chewing.

What’s inside your vitamins?

Only the good stuff. Every ingredient is purposefully chosen, backed by research, and free from artificial colours, flavours, and sweeteners. Sweetness comes from monk fruit and thaumatin—nothing fake here.

How do I update or cancel my subscription?

You’re in control—log in, click ‘manage your subscription’, and you can pause, skip, swap, or cancel anytime (no lock-in contracts or secret handshakes).

But hey—if your kid’s gone on a vitamin strike or your routine’s hit a speed bump, email us at support@koakids.com.au. We’ve got tricks, tips, and tasty ways to help you stick with the good stuff, or just a solution to make things more “you.”

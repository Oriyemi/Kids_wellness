    FAQs

    Is Tummy Time vegan?

    Yes! Tummy Time is 100% vegan. None of the ingredients come from animals, and no animal-derived materials are used at any point in sourcing or manufacturing.

    Why does gut health matter for kids?

    Gut health plays an important role in digestion, immune function, and overall wellbeing. Tummy Time supports digestive and gut flora balance with a high-quality pre + probiotic blend—featuring 20 diverse probiotic strains to help nourish kids’ microbiome diversity.

    How do I give Tummy Time to my child?

    Most kids benefit from daily use but always check with your healthcare provider if you’re managing a specific gut issue or medical condition.

    One little sachet a day is all it takes to keep those good gut bugs happy. Just mix it into water, juice, yoghurt, or sprinkle right onto a spoon. It even works straight from the sachet—no drama and no fridge required. But please keep it out of hot drinks so the probiotics stay alive and well.immune system a little extra muscle.

    Why is Tummy Time a powder instead of a chewable or gummy?

    Because chewables and gummies are rough on probiotics. They involve heat and pressure—bad news for delicate bacteria. Powder keeps the good bugs safe and ready to do their job.

    What makes KOA Kids’ probiotic different?

    Tummy Time delivers 10 billion CFU across 20 diverse strains, plus prebiotics like inulin and banana resistant starch to keep the good bacteria well-fed. No fridge needed, no fillers, just targeted gut support that works.

    Can I use Tummy Time daily?

    Yes! Tummy Time is formulated for daily use to support digestive system health and immune function.

    Can Tummy Time help with digestion?

    Tummy Time is designed to support digestive system health and healthy bowel function. If your child is experiencing specific symptoms or digestive concerns, it’s best to consult your healthcare professional for individual guidance.
